Emotional Intelligence


What is Emotional Intelligence?

Basically emotional intelligence is being able to understand your own emotions and manage them in a positive way. These positive ways help you deal with stress and help you build stronger relationships with other people


How is EQ different to IQ?


IQ is raw intelligence , like logical problem solving and being able to understand ideas. Where as EQ is more emotional and allows one to better at understanding others, your surrounding and resolving conflicts with others. Stuff like that

Why is EQ important?


As stated it allows one to manage their impulses a lot more effectively and coming up with ways to deal with stress, It should also be noted that getting along with others is paramount in society , so EQ is pretty important.
